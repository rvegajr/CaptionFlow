Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-05.

What we collect

  • Account data: when you sign in, we store your email address, an institution association if you arrived via LTI, and a server-side session.
  • Caption content: the SRT/VTT files you upload, the YouTube video IDs they reference, and metadata about caption-sharing grants you create.
  • View metering: when an embedded caption resource is loaded, we log a timestamped view_event tied to your institution. We do not log individual viewer identities.
  • Operational logs: request logs (URL, status code, latency) and error traces to keep the service running.

What we do not collect

  • We do not sell or rent any personal data.
  • We do not place third-party advertising trackers on our pages.
  • We do not log video-watch progress for individual viewers.

Third parties

  • YouTube: the video player is loaded from YouTube and is governed by YouTube's own privacy policy.
  • Resend: we use Resend to deliver magic-link sign-in emails.
  • DeepL / Google Cloud Translation: if you request a machine translation, the source caption text is sent to the configured provider.
  • Railway: our application and PostgreSQL database are hosted on Railway.

Your rights

You may request export or deletion of your account data at any time by emailing support@captionflow.link. For institution-managed accounts, please also contact your institution's CaptionFlow administrator.

Contact

Questions: support@captionflow.link.
